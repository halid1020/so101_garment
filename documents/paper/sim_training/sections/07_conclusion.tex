\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

We built a training environment inside our rig's digital twin that
proves the entire imitation-learning loop before any real demonstration
exists: contact-based single-arm and bimanual-relay tasks, a scripted
oracle that demonstrates through the full production teleoperation
pipeline via a closed-form inversion of its retargeting, closed-loop
corrections that stand in for the operator's eyes, and gated collection
that admits only verified successes under a leak-proof seed protocol.
The difficulties catalogue records every failure met on the way ---
solver steady-state error defeating small-object grasps, correction
wind-up and dragging through pipeline lag, an envelope floor silently
rejecting table-level targets, and a series of training-stack pitfalls
--- so that the environment can be rebuilt without rediscovering them.
The experiments section will grow with the living document as the full
campaign runs; the next step beyond it is the one the environment was
built to de-risk: collecting real teleoperated demonstrations on the
physical rig and finetuning the same policy families on them.
